This application is developed for employers to manage their workforce easily. This app allows Human Resources department and Admins to manage companies employees' attendance and all their data.

First you have to register yourself and fill out all the details on our registration page. After that you have to wait for your approval by admins and after getting aproved your login ID and password will be sent on your registered email.

To get your attendance marked everyday you have to login at your office arrival and when you logout you will be mark as present in the system. Your active time will be visible to the admins.

After you login you can access tasks given to you and check all your pasat attendance data to this data.
